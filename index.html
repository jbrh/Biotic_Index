Determining Overall Quality Of Stream Water Using A Biotic Index

This worksheet provides additional practice determining the biotic index of a stream through a virtual stream study of macroinvertebrates.

Group 1 --Highly Sensitive to Pollution:      Stonefly Dobsonfly

Group 2 --Semi-Sensitive to Pollution:      Caddisfly Dragonfly Waterpenny Crawfish Cranefly Damselfly Mayfly


Group 3 -- Semi-Tolerant of Pollution:      Orb Snail Scud

Group 4 -- Tolerant of Pollution:      Sowbug Leech Tubefix worm Pouch Snail



You have just dredged the streambed using this kicknet :)    Carefully drag leaves and rocks away to reveal macros. Using the guide above, segregate macros into different types near the margins of the kicknet. Count the number of types (not number of individuals) per pollution tolerance Group, then submit your data and simple calculated values inot the table to the left to determine overall stream health.
